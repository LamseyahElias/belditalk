# Belditalk.com 30-Day Social Media Calendar

Audience: English and French speakers who want practical Moroccan Darija for travel, family, work, culture, or living in Morocco.

Core content pillars:
- Survival Darija: greetings, shopping, taxis, restaurants, directions.
- Pronunciation: sounds that learners struggle with, especially `kh`, `gh`, `q`, `3`, and emphatic consonants.
- Culture: etiquette, hospitality, Ramadan, hammam, souks, family language, Moroccan humor.
- Confidence: short dialogues, mistakes to avoid, repeat-after-me practice.

Posting rhythm:
- Instagram: 3 posts per week, using a mix of Reels, carousels, and story prompts.
- TikTok: 2 short videos per week, focused on fast hooks and repeatable phrases.
- YouTube: 1 longer video per month, optimized for search and beginner value.

## TikTok Creative Formula

Use this structure for most TikToks:
1. Hook in first 2 seconds: "Say this in Morocco, not textbook Arabic."
2. Show phrase on screen in Darija transliteration.
3. Say it slowly, then naturally.
4. Give one realistic context.
5. Ask for a repeat, duet, comment, or save.

Recommended TikTok length:
- Pronunciation drills: 12-20 seconds.
- Mini dialogues: 20-35 seconds.
- Culture facts: 15-30 seconds.
