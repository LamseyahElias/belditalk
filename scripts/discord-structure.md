# Belditalk.com Discord Server Structure

Purpose: give Moroccan Darija learners a clear, friendly place to practice, ask questions, receive announcements, and build consistent speaking habits.

## Server Settings

Server name: Belditalk Darija Academy

Default language: English, with Darija practice encouraged in learning channels.

Verification level:
- Medium for public launches.
- Require verified email.
- Enable Discord AutoMod for spam, slurs, invite links, and repeated messages.

Membership screening:
- Require agreement to rules.
- Ask one onboarding question: "What is your Darija level?"
- Options: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Moroccan Heritage Learner, Just Exploring.

## Channel Categories and Channels

### Start Here

#welcome
- Purpose: first stop for new members.
- Content: welcome message, quick introduction prompt, links to rules and beginner channel.
- Permissions: everyone can read and post short introductions.

#rules
- Purpose: server rules and community expectations.
- Permissions: read-only for members.
- Suggested rules:
  1. Be respectful and patient with learners at every level.
  2. No harassment, hate speech, mocking accents, or shaming mistakes.
  3. Keep religious, political, and sensitive cultural topics respectful and relevant.
  4. Do not spam, self-promote, or post unrelated links.
  5. Use the right channel for your question or practice.
  6. Do not share paid course materials outside the approved spaces.
  7. Protect privacy. Do not repost voice notes, screenshots, or personal information without consent.

#announcements
- Purpose: course updates, live sessions, new videos, discounts, weekly challenges.
- Permissions: read-only for members; Admin and Instructor can post.

### Learn Darija

#beginner-darija
- Purpose: absolute beginner questions and simple phrase practice.
- Example prompts:
  - "Write one sentence using `bghit`."
  - "Ask how someone is using Darija."
  - "Share one word you heard but did not understand."

#intermediate-darija
- Purpose: longer sentences, listening questions, grammar patterns, daily-life scenarios.
- Example prompts:
  - "Describe your day using 5 Darija verbs."
  - "Translate a cafe or taxi conversation."
  - "Ask about word order or tense patterns."

#advanced-darija
- Purpose: idioms, slang, fast speech, regional variation, transcription practice.
- Example prompts:
  - "Explain this proverb or expression."
  - "Transcribe a short Moroccan audio clip."
  - "Compare Casablanca, Rabat, Fes, and Marrakech usage."

#pronunciation-practice
- Purpose: voice notes and pronunciation feedback.
- Weekly focus examples:
  - Week 1: `kh` sound.
  - Week 2: `gh` sound.
  - Week 3: `3` sound.
  - Week 4: emphatic consonants.
- Rule: feedback must be constructive and specific.

#weekly-challenges
- Purpose: structured weekly practice tasks.
- Challenge examples:
  - Record a 20-second self-introduction.
  - Order tea in Darija.
  - Use five words from this week's vocabulary list.
  - Translate a short Moroccan family dialogue.

#resources
- Purpose: approved learning materials.
- Content:
  - Belditalk course links.
  - Free phrase sheets.
  - YouTube videos.
  - Pronunciation guides.
  - Recommended dictionaries or keyboards.
- Permissions: Admin, Instructor, and Moderator can post; members can react.

### Culture and Community

#cultural-corner
- Purpose: Moroccan culture, etiquette, holidays, food, music, family language, travel tips.
- Example prompts:
  - "When should I say `tbark Allah`?"
  - "What should I know before visiting a Moroccan home?"
  - "What does this Moroccan expression mean culturally?"

#off-topic
- Purpose: casual conversation that does not fit learning channels.
- Boundaries: no spam, no heated debates, no unrelated promotion.

## Roles

Admin:
- Elias and core operations team.
- Full server permissions.
- Manages roles, channels, bots, announcements, and incidents.

Instructor:
- Teachers and approved Darija coaches.
- Can post in announcements, resources, and weekly challenges.
- Can host live sessions and give corrections.

Moderator:
- Enforces rules, handles reports, removes spam, manages threads.
- Can timeout members when needed.

Course Student:
- Paid Belditalk course member.
- Access to student-only events or channels if added later.
- Can receive special practice prompts and office hour links.

Community Member:
- Default approved member role.
- Can access learning and community channels.

Beginner:
- Self-selected learner level.
- Helps instructors route questions and prompts.

Intermediate:
- Self-selected learner level.
- Used for level-specific challenges.

Advanced:
- Self-selected learner level.
- Used for higher-level discussions and transcription challenges.

Heritage Learner:
- Learners with Moroccan family or background who may understand some Darija but want speaking confidence.

Event Guest:
- Temporary role for webinar attendees or public live sessions.

Muted:
- Restricted role for rule violations.
- Cannot post or join voice channels.

## Welcome Message

Post in #welcome:

Welcome to the Belditalk Darija Academy.

I am Elias Lamseyah, founder of Belditalk.com. This community is here to help you learn Moroccan Darija through practical phrases, pronunciation practice, culture, and consistent speaking.

Start here:

1. Read #rules.
2. Choose your level role: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Heritage Learner.
3. Introduce yourself in this channel.
4. Post your first Darija question in #beginner-darija.

Introduction template:

Salam, smiti [your name].  
Ana mn [country/city].  
Bghit nt3llm Darija hit [your reason].

English:

Hello, my name is [your name].  
I am from [country/city].  
I want to learn Darija because [your reason].

You do not need perfect spelling or pronunciation. Start simple, practice often, and be respectful to other learners.

Mrehba bikom.

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

Monday:
- Post one phrase challenge in #weekly-challenges.

Tuesday:
- Share a pronunciation prompt in #pronunciation-practice.

Wednesday:
- Post a cultural question in #cultural-corner.

Thursday:
- Instructor replies to top learner questions.

Friday:
- Share a short quiz or listening prompt.

Sunday:
- Recap the week's best answers and announce the next challenge.

## Moderation Playbook

First issue:
- Friendly reminder in channel or DM.

Repeated issue:
- Formal warning and link to rules.

Serious issue:
- Timeout, delete message, document in #mod-log.

Severe harassment, hate speech, doxxing, or piracy:
- Immediate ban.

## Success Metrics

Track weekly:
- New members.
- Introductions posted.
- Active learners in practice channels.
- Voice notes submitted.
- Weekly challenge completions.
- Course clicks from Discord.
- Support questions resolved.
